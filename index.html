How it works

Math × Computer Science for Swim Training

Analyze swim splits from video or image data and find pace drop-off patterns.

BlueLane turns raw race evidence into clean split analytics. Use frame-based event detection (video) or image OCR fallback (where available) to estimate lap timing, then compute consistency, trend slope, and algorithmic confidence.
